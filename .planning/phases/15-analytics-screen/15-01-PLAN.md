    INJECTED calendar's timezone (defaults to `.current` = device timezone), not a hardcoded `TimeZone.current`.
    Do NOT change the public `bucket(...)` signature, the `SpendRange`/`SpendBucket` types, the self-transfer
    filter (`$0.isTransfer != true`), or the zero-slot generation (Pitfall C). The default `calendar: .current`
