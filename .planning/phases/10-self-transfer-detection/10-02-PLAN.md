<objective>
Wire the confirmed-transfer spend/budget/chart exclusion (XFER-04, D-15). Confirmed self-transfers must be absent from every spend total, budget progress bar, and chart — visible only under the dedicated Transfers filter (built in Plan 03). The exclusion is a single `.filter { $0.isTransfer != true }` applied at each aggregator's input boundary. `OverviewAggregation` needs no direct math change (it consumes `BudgetCalculator` output) but must be verified to never receive a raw unfiltered `[Expense]` array.

Purpose: Without this, a confirmed transfer (e.g. ₹50,000 moved between accounts) would inflate spend and budget progress, defeating the entire feature. This plan is independent of the scorer — it filters on the already-existing `isTransfer` field, so it runs in parallel with Plan 01.
Output: Modified BudgetCalculator, SpendOverTimeAggregator, verified OverviewAggregation, and exclusion tests.
</objective>
