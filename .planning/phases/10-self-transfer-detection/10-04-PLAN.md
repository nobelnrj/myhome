<objective>
Add manual mark/unmark of any expense as a self-transfer (XFER-05, D-14) on the expense edit view. Marking sets a SOLO transfer flag (`isTransfer = true`, `transferPairID` left as-is/nil). Unmarking resets to `nil` (so the scorer can re-evaluate it) and cascade-unlinks any counterpart (fetch the partner by `transferPairID` and reset it too). The toggle uses the established `@State` local-mirror form pattern and participates in `isDirty`.

Purpose: Auto-detection misses or misfires (D-06 acknowledges refund ambiguity); the manual escape hatch lets the user mark a missed transfer or unmark a misfire. A solo transfer is excluded from spend (Plan 02's `!= true` filter already handles it) but does not move balance (D-16 — no counterpart). This plan touches only EditExpenseView, so it runs in parallel with Plans 01-03.
Output: Modified EditExpenseView with a transfer toggle + cascade-unlink, and unit tests.
</objective>
